## Tips & Tricks

- **Individual Control**: You can pause each reminder type independently while keeping others active
- **Custom Intervals**: Adjust reminder intervals based on your work schedule
- **Test First**: Use Test Notifications to ensure notifications work before relying on reminders
- **View Logs**: Check logs to track reminder history

## Notification Behavior

When a reminder triggers:

- A Windows toast notification appears (top-right)
- Sound plays (if enabled)
- Notification stays visible until dismissed or expires
- You can click to dismiss early
